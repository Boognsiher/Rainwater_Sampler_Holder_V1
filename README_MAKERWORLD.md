# 🌧️ Rainwater Sampler Holder – Funnel & Sample Vial Mount

A 3D-printable mount designed to hold a standard funnel and a laboratory vial for automatic rainwater collection. Built for research use, but straightforward enough for any maker to build and deploy outdoors.

The holder consists of two halves that clamp around the vial, with an integrated millimetre scale (10–60 mm) visible through a viewing window on the side. A hinged arm holds the funnel in place at the top.

---

## 🖨️ Printing

The model contains **38 bodies** split into 4 groups:

| Part | Bodies | Colour |
|---|---|---|
| Holder – right half | 1 | Main colour |
| Holder – left half | 1 | Main colour |
| Cap | 1 | Main colour |
| Scale markings (TextR + TextL) | 18 + 18 = 36 | Contrast colour |

> **Multi-material required for scale markings:** The scale characters (digits and dashes) are modelled as individual bodies so they can be printed in a contrasting colour. This requires an **AMS, MMU, or similar multi-material system**. Manual filament swaps per character are technically possible but very tedious. Printing the scale in a single colour is also fine – the markings will still be readable.
